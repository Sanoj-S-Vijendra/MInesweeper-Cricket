Instructions

1. Welcome! to this remix of minesweeper and cricket.
2. Basic Rules are same as that of classic minesweeper.
3. But, as cricket is mixed, runs are distrubuted randomly on board.
4. User can score 1,2,3,4 or 6 runs in each turn.
5. Beware! of fielders, they have occupied their places blindy on the field.
6. Numbers of fielders on the board depends on the size of grid-board, so are the number of wickets.
7. Score as many runs as you can before you run out of wickets and indulge yourself in happiness when you see your name on the leaderboard.
8. Enjoy! the Game.
